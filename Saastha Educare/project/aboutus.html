About Us

SAASTHA EDUCARE was born out of the dream of three directors who had a vision to make a modest contribution to society. SAASTHA has been working towards its mission to enable students realize their potential and make their dreams come true. At SAASTHA the preparation process itself is a fun filled and growth involving experience.  

What makes Saastha different ?

The efficiency of any institute's teaching standard is determined by one thing its approach. We value our students and their success. We realize that the success of our students is the only reason for our existence. And since this is our driving force, we lay strong emphasis on certain cardinal principles:

  • We are a teaching organization with focus on good teaching above everything else.
  • We strive to fulfil the needs of our students to the best of our capabilities.
  • We work really hard, setting high standards and expect the same from our students too.
  • We inform and educate our students about the need for strong self discipline and self motivation to achieve their goals.

From Director's desk ...

Indian education taught the world everything...from the difference between loss and profit, to the rise and fall of Economy, a glimpse to the beauty of nature, and to the disasters to life...With great contributors like Ramanujan and CV Raman, we have proved the world that we are proud to be a part of the richest educational hub.

We, the beginners of Gurukul System, wished to reside with the Guru (Teacher) and simultaneously gain knowledge. Building a personal relationship with the teacher, we sought enlightment on various aspects of life. But today, it is a different picture all together. One side, there are number of students (not so attentive!—you could add if you want to) and on the other side we have a teacher making an effort to provide quality education.

The gap you see in between both is not just a physical gap, but a personal one too. Thanks to this professional way of educating, where a student is losing the personal attachment with the teacher. We at Saastha, aim to change this scenario, preparing a student for a real life. Educate in a way where a student has an ability to think by himself and explore the world around him/her. We build in situations where in the student is made to think and handle the circumstances in a positive manner. Emphasizing on mind set development, we not only wish to help the student in honing his/her knowledge but also intend to create a strong tie between teacher and student.

After all, Knowledge is not books you carry, grades you get, it is the ability to find your own answers!
