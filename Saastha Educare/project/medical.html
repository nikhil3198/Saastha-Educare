The All India Pre-Medical Test (AIPMT) or National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is an annual medical entrance examination held in India.. Conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Delhi the exam is conducted for seeking admission to BDS and MBBS courses in top medical colleges all over the country.

The exam consists of a single paper with 180 objective type questions from subjects including Physics, Biology (Botany & Zoology) and Chemistry. From each subject students are asked 45 questions which they have to complete within a duration of 3 hours. 4 marks are allotted for each question. For incorrect response there is one negative marking. However, if a student does not attend any question, no marks will be deducted.

Through NEET training offered by Deeksha, students excel in medical entrance exam, which helps them secure a seat in top medical colleges all over India. The trained and experienced faculty at Deeksha make sure the students understand the concept clearly before attempting to answer the question.
